About Mel

Melchior (“Mel”) Busschers is an executive broadcast engineer and Outside Broadcast (OB) leader based in the UAE, with over 40 years of international delivery across live sport, ceremonies, concerts, and high-profile productions. His focus is simple: reliable live broadcast operations — the people, the technology, and the workflows that keep complex shows running under pressure.

Through MelBroadcast Productions, Mel supports clients with OB engineering leadership, technical management, and systems integration — from planning and design to commissioning, training, and show-day readiness. He is comfortable bridging engineering teams, production stakeholders, vendors, and broadcasters to deliver stable results.
